Getting blocked with a Simple Problem.

The problem was I wasn't able to locate what was wrong with a part of the code I was working on and it kept coming up with an error!

I first googled if anyone had had any similar problems, and then asked my peers.

I felt a bit overwhelmed and very frustrated because I couldn't source the problem right away and I was trying to keep within a certain time frame.

I learnt, NOT TO PANIC, and then take a deep breath, read over the code again and ask your peers first because they will be going through the same problems.

Solved a problem in an elegant way.

My blog wasn't appearing online and I had no idea why!

I googled first trying to see why it wasn't online, I couldn't find any answers, then I asked my peers and they couldn't figure it out, then my tutor suggested an option, and I tried it and it didn't fully work, so I ended up fully moving my blog to another place on my computer, on the back of what my tutor said, and it worked!

Very frustrated and like a failure because it was one of the first things that we were doing in the course.

Again, to trust yourself and your ability and ask for help and that everyone is in the same boat at the beggining.
